Admin Nuvem

A redesign of Tiendanube/Nuvemshop administrator, understanding carefully the needs of its users and how to create a better experience for a product that more than 30.000 users use every day.



Role:

Product designer. Understanding the users. Defining the scope. Thinking the UX. Designing the UI. Coding.


Understanding

01

User Interviews

Performed user interviews to understand their pain points and oportunities.

02

Card Sorting

Conducted card-sorting excercises with over 30 users to rethink the product architecture.

03

Data Analysis

Gathered and analyzed data to understand how the users used the platform.

04

Business mindset

Necessary research to understand bussiness decisions and the vision of the product.

Design

1. Reorganization of the product arquitecture

Based on the feedback and information gathered, I worked on the creation of a new arichitecture for the platform. The objective was to create something aligned with the users mindset, so that it would make sense for them where to find each thing. Also it focused on creating a user flow that enabled maximum optimization depending on the way the users used the platform.

2. Creation of low definition prototypes

Created low definition prototypes to define well the user flows, together with some UI decisions. First, I worked on pen and paper to experiment many different paths easily and effectively. Then I moved on to creating the wireframes, first very basics and then more detailed, in order to be able to test them fast.

3. Testing with users

Used invision and maze to understand if the new UX and UI made sense for our users. In this way we were able to test and iterate, before starting to code.

Implement

01

Defining the MVP

Decided which parts were essential to deliver value to our users in less than 6 weeks.

02

Code (front end)

Coded most of the front end for the new UI.

03

Rollout and feedback

Participated in the rollout process, and asked users for feedback in order to keep itarating.
